Pasta

Description

This homemade pasta recipe is consistently great and easy to make with flour, eggs, salt, olive oil, and water.

Roll out by hand or use a pasta machine to cut dough into desired pasta shape.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Turn the dough out and knead it for 10 minutes
  3. Let the dough rest
  4. Divide the dough into balls
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes
